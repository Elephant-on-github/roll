# Plan
there will be a defense stage and attack stage
denoted by a sword or shield icon

there will be opponents taken from / based on kenny.nl

it will be based arround dice roll+modifiers
3(+1) types:
	debuff - makes your opponent worse
	both - makes both weaker e.g make all 6s 2s
	buff - makes self stronger all 5s 7s
	(secret type) - something special e.g if two twos rolled square attack damage

you can get more dice or a modifier after every round

the opponents will have scaling health that is multiplier (exponential or quadratic) x random number 1,10


the ui will have a wimsical 2d theme with some animation
